How to use the manual :



1) In the table of contents click on any chapter, section or subsection to

   navigate directly to that page.


2) While reading the steps of any procedure, click on the figure number to

   directly jump to the figure.


3) Click on any URL to visit the corresponding webpage.


4) In chapter 4 (Experiments) click on any section number to navigate to

   that experiment directly.


5) Click on any reference number (eg. [1],[2]...) to go the reference page.



Have Fun !
